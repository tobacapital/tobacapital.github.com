Toba Capital is a new venture firm founded by Vinny Smith. We are a small team with double the per capita operating experience of traditional VCs, investing in the markets we know firsthand: enterprise software and IT infrastructure.

Our background enables us to provide portfolio companies with unique operational support. We know what it takes to build a billion dollar software company and have seen both sides of over a hundred investments and M&A transactions.

It is a time of seismic change for the enterprise, and innovators are continually exposing new bedrock. We back entrepreneurs who map the fissures and are quick to build on still-shaking ground.

Our diligence process is a collaborative exploration of three major variables: your team, traction, and our opportunity to add value. We will never have a ‘standard’ investment profile; for businesses with transformative potential we believe that one size fits less than none.
